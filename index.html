Vision

"To be center for quality education and research, through excellent academic ambience and natural relation with society and industry, with an objective of purposeful existence in society."

Mission

"We, at ATSS regard it as our mission to develop competent professionals & entrepreneurs capable of withstanding and managing the ever-changing scenario in the world of Information Technology & Management, and having a deep-rooted sense of social responsibility."

Objective

  • To promote research and development in Information Technology and Management related areas among faculty members and students.
  • To provide professional education of excellent quality affordable to all by imparting knowledge ,skill and ability.
  • To develop strong linkage between Industry and academia by understanding critical needs of the industry and providing competent manpower.
  • To involve and empower employees to enhance their satisfaction levels.
  • To imbibe an ethical and deep rooted sense of social responsibility in teaching-learning fraternity.
